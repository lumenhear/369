Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if I have hearing loss?

Common signs include difficulty hearing conversations in noisy places, asking people to repeat themselves, turning up the TV volume, and experiencing ringing in your ears (tinnitus). The best way to know for sure is to get a professional hearing test.

Are modern hearing aids noticeable?

Technology has advanced significantly. Many modern hearing aids are incredibly discreet, with some models being completely invisible when worn. We offer a wide range of styles to suit your needs and cosmetic preferences.

How long does speech therapy take?

The duration of speech therapy varies greatly depending on the individual's condition, age, and goals. After an initial assessment, our therapists will create a personalized plan and give you an estimated timeline.

Is a hearing test painful?

Not at all. A comprehensive hearing test is a completely painless and non-invasive procedure. You will listen to a series of sounds through headphones in a soundproof booth and respond accordingly.
