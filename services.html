Our Areas of Expertise

Advanced, personalized care for every aspect of your hearing and speech health.

Speech Therapy

Personalized programs for children and adults covering articulation, fluency (stuttering), voice therapy, and language disorders.

Diagnostic Audiology

Comprehensive testing including Pure Tone Audiometry (PTA), OAE, BERA, and Impedance for accurate diagnosis.

Fitting, trials, and servicing of digital, rechargeable, and invisible hearing aids from top global brands.

Cochlear Implant Support

Expert mapping, rehabilitation, and ongoing support for cochlear implant users to maximize their hearing potential.

Pediatric Hearing Care

Specialized care for children, including newborn hearing screening, early intervention, and child-friendly testing.

Counseling & Aural Rehabilitation

Guidance and therapy to help patients and families adapt to hearing loss and hearing devices effectively.

Tinnitus Management

Personalized strategies and sound therapies to help manage and reduce the impact of tinnitus (ringing in the ears).

Balance / Vestibular Testing

Advanced diagnostic testing to identify the causes of dizziness, vertigo, and balance disorders.
